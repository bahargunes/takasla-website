How It Works

Exchange items in four simple steps

1. Create a Listing

Snap a photo and add a brief description of the item you want to trade.

2. Browse Items

Explore items offered by others in your community that you might want.

3. Match & Exchange

Propose a trade. If both parties agree, it's a match! No money involved.

4. Complete Trade

Meet up or ship the items to finalize the exchange securely and easily.
