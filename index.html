Our Vision

Traditional bartering relies on unstructured and inefficient processes. We are modernizing the barter ecosystem by enabling structured item exchange, improving trust and fairness, and using AI to simplify and optimize trading. Takasla is an engineered solution for a sustainable digital exchange.

View the Flow

Platform Workflow

Structured item exchange process

1. Create a Listing

Snap a photo and add a brief description of the item you want to trade.

2. Browse Items

Explore items offered by others in your community that you might want.

3. Match & Exchange

Propose a trade. If both parties agree, it's a match! No money involved.

4. Complete Trade

Meet up or ship the items to finalize the exchange securely and easily.

Features

Everything you need for seamless bartering

AI Value Matching

Estimates trade fairness using item metadata, category similarity, and user-defined preferences.

AI Category Detection

Utilizes Gemini Vision and image classification models to automatically categorize items, streamlining the listing workflow.

Smart Recommendations

Provides personalized recommendations based on browsing activity, wishlist interactions, exchange history, and user preferences.

Rating & Badge System

Build trust through ratings and badges that reflect successful trades and community engagement.

Bundle Swap Offers

Combine multiple items into a single trade offer to create fair and flexible exchanges.

In-app Chat

Communicate and negotiate trades securely with real-time messaging inside the platform.
